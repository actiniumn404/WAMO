Why WAMO?

Our Vision

For us at WAMO, math is an exciting adventure in problem-solving and understanding the world. We're building a welcoming community where students in Washington can discover the joy of math, push their limits, and grow their skills.

What Sets Us Apart

  • We're all about making math fun and understandable for everyone, no matter where they're starting
  • We offer organized ways to learn through our contests, which are all designed for all levels
  • Our goal is to help young mathematicians feel confident and really good at math

Our Expertise

  • We're a team of successful students who know math competitions inside and out (think JMO, AMO)
  • We also have experience writing problems for many different competitions (JMPSC, MM, and more)
  • Plus, we have experienced mentors providing high-quality problems and guidance
