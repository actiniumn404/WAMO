Math for the Evergreen State WAMO: Uniting Washington’s math minds through challenge, community, and creativity.

Projects

WAMO contests are designed to challenge and inspire math students. From creative problem-solving to rigorous proofs, each competition offers a unique opportunity for students to test their skills and deepen their love for mathematics. Whether you're new to contests or a seasoned competitor, WAMO events foster a supportive community and a spirit of curiosity.

Why WAMO?

"Without mathematics there is no art."
- Luca Pacioli

Our Vision

  • At WAMO, we believe mathematics transcends mere numbers—it's a journey of deep thinking, problem-solving, and shaping tomorrow. We're building a vibrant community where Washington students can discover, challenge, and elevate their mathematical abilities.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Dedicated to making mathematics accessible and engaging for all skill levels
  • Structured learning through contests, potential classes, and interactive activities
  • Focus on building both confidence and competence in young mathematicians

Our Expertise

  • Team of accomplished students with extensive competition experience (JMO, AMO)
  • Problem-writing experience for various competitions (JMPSC, MM, and more)
  • Experienced mentors providing high-quality problems and guidance

Meet the Team

We are experienced competitive mathematicians who have scored well in contests ranging from MATHCOUNTS to the USAMO. Our team is comprised of multiple MATHCOUNTS National qualifiers, AMC 8 and AMC 10 perfect scorers, AIME top scorers, and USA(J)MO participants.
